Who We Are

K-Soft Company WLL is an IT Consulting & Systems Integration Company, a fully owned subsidiary of Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo Group, it is one of the largest independent, family owned, group of companies in the Gulf region. K-Soft was first established in the Dubai Internet City in 2001 and provides Laboratory Information Management Systems from Thermo Scientific (Informatics div), Chromatography Data Systems, Thin Print Solutions, Systems Integration Services and Software Development projects for the region. This success of the operation is evident from an impressive clientele that includes Takreer – UAE, GASCO – UAE, Borouge – UAE, Oman Refinery – Oman, PDO – Oman, BAPCO – Bahrain, Saudi Aramco - KSA, SABIC group – KSA, QP-Qatar, MELUBCO – UAE, Dolphin Energy – UAE & Mitsui OSK Lines – UAE.
K-Soft is able to draw upon the experience, knowhow and business acumen of the YBA Kanoo Group that spans 130 years and also the YBA Kanoo IT organization that has been in existence since 40 years, the Group was quick to embrace Information Technology in all its business activities as early as the 1960's. It is this know-how, skill sets and processes that have helped it develop, implement and support diverse IT projects both within the Kanoo enterprise as well as at a number of customers in the region. K-Soft is able to leverage a large resource pool that is based in Bahrain, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Egypt as well as at Gemini operations in India.
As a part of the regional expansion plan, K-Soft commenced its Bahrain operations in June 2007 to cater to clients in Saudi Arabia, Qatar as well as Bahrain.
