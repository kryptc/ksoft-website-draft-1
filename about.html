ABOUT K-SOFT

K-Soft Company WLL is an IT Consulting & Systems Integration Company, a fully owned subsidiary of Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo Group, it is one of the largest independent, family owned, group of companies in the Gulf region. Established in Bahrain in 1890 by Haji Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo, the group is a diversified business conglomerate with business interests across the world's most dynamic industries in Shipping, Travel, Holidays, Information Technology, Machinery, Oil & Gas, Power & Industrial Projects to Exhibition services, Courier Services, Logistics, Specialty Chemicals and Business Centers and other retail and commercial activities. Visit www.ybakanoo.com for more details.

Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo is the first Bahraini company to establish a 100% export-oriented IT Enabled services company - Gemini Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd which commenced operations in July 1998 and is based in Technopark, Trivandrum, India, one of the leading software technology parks in the world. A new state-of-the-art development centre in Mumbai, India will be operational in few months.

K-Soft was first established in the Dubai Internet City in 2001 and provides Laboratory Information Management Systems from Thermo Scientific (Informatics div), Chromatography Data Systems, Thin Print Solutions, Systems Integration Services and Software Development projects for the region. This success of the operation is evident from an impressive clientele that includes Takreer – UAE, GASCO – UAE, Borouge – UAE, Oman Refinery – Oman, PDO – Oman, BAPCO – Bahrain, Saudi Aramco - KSA, SABIC group – KSA, QP-Qatar, MELUBCO – UAE, Dolphin Energy – UAE & Mitsui OSK Lines – UAE.
Visit www.ksoft.ae for more details.

K-Soft is able to draw upon the experience, knowhow and business acumen of the YBA Kanoo Group that spans 130 years and also the YBA Kanoo IT organization that has been in existence since 40 years, the Group was quick to embrace Information Technology in all its business activities as early as the 1960's. It is this know-how, skill sets and processes that have helped it develop, implement and support diverse IT projects both within the Kanoo enterprise as well as at a number of customers in the region. K-Soft is able to leverage a large resource pool that is based in Bahrain, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Egypt as well as at Gemini operations in India.

As a part of the regional expansion plan, K-Soft commenced its Bahrain operations in June 2007 to cater to clients in Saudi Arabia, Qatar as well as Bahrain.

K-Soft derives its value systems from its parent - The Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo Group. While today The Kanoo Group stands as a modern symbol of growth and diversification, the core of the group remains true to the traditional business values Haji Yusuf Bin Ahmed Kanoo practiced over a hundred years ago – the values of ethics, respect, fair-play and compassion for humanity.

It is this combination of modernity with tradition, cutting edge innovation with the human touch and global standards with local know-how that makes up the unique personality of our group and its subsidiary companies.
